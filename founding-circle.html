What The Founding Circle Is

Five members. No more. That is the entire idea.

Heritage Union is built on personal attention — James and Huyen involved in every stage, for every client. The Founding Circle takes that principle further still, reserved for a small number of men who want the closest possible relationship with the people running this consultancy, from the earliest days of its history.

Membership is not something you can simply purchase online. It is discussed, and offered, during your private consultation — because it only works if it remains genuinely small.

What Membership Includes

Enhanced access. Lasting standing.

Every Heritage Union client receives founder involvement and Huyen's personal screening. Founding Circle members receive more of it, and are recognised accordingly.

Enhanced founder access

More direct time with James throughout your journey — not limited to the initial consultation and scheduled updates.

Priority attention

Your search, screening and introductions are given priority handling by James and Huyen personally.

Recognition

Founding Circle members are acknowledged as part of Heritage Union's earliest and most trusted relationships — a standing that does not change as the consultancy grows.

Early access

First consideration for any new service, region or refinement to the Heritage Union process as the consultancy develops.
