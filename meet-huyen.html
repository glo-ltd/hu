Her Story

Vietnam, from the inside.

Huyen was raised in Vietnam, in a family that placed loyalty, respect and togetherness above everything else. Those values still shape how she works with every candidate and every client today.

Long before Heritage Union existed, Huyen had spent years helping friends and family navigate the space between Vietnamese tradition and the expectations of the wider world — translating not just language, but intention.

"A good introduction isn't about who looks right together. It's about who understands each other's world — I make sure that understanding starts before two people ever meet."

Working alongside James

When James began imagining a consultancy built on trust rather than volume, he needed to work with someone who could see both sides of the bridge clearly — someone Vietnamese women could trust completely, and someone Western clients could rely on for an honest, unfiltered view.

Huyen is that person. As an independent cultural expert and liaison, she leads every stage of the process that happens on the ground in Vietnam: meeting candidates in person, understanding their circumstances and intentions, and preparing them for what a genuine cross-cultural relationship will actually involve.

What she looks for

Huyen's approach goes far beyond a photograph and a form. She looks for women who are serious about building a genuine, long-term relationship and family, grounded in their own independence, and honest about what they want from a future partner.

She is equally direct with clients — if a match isn't right, she says so. That honesty, more than anything else, is what clients say they value most.

Cultural guidance, throughout

Beyond candidate matching, Huyen is the voice clients turn to when a conversation gets lost in translation — not of language, but of expectation. Family obligations, the pace of a serious relationship, the role of parents in a relationship, or simply how to show respect in a new culture.

Her guidance continues long after an introduction is made, through every stage of a relationship's development toward a genuine, lasting future together.

Her Role at Heritage Union

Local expertise, applied with care.

Every candidate a client is introduced to has been through Huyen's process — in person, not on a spreadsheet.

01

Candidate matching

Rather than leaving clients to sift through endless profiles, Huyen searches every opted-in candidate, identifies the strongest matches for their preferences, and presents a curated shortlist — meeting each candidate in person to assess intention, character and readiness for a serious, long-term relationship before anyone is introduced.

02

Cultural guidance

Helping clients understand Vietnamese family life, values and expectations — and helping candidates understand life with a Western partner.

03

Relationship support

A steady, honest presence as a relationship develops — bridging language, distance and the everyday realities of a cross-cultural future.

04

Vietnam-side concierge

Coordinating visits, introductions and family meetings on the ground in Vietnam, so clients arrive prepared and welcomed.

"I am proud to be Vietnamese, and I am proud of every candidate who trusts us with her future. My job is to protect that trust — for her, and for the man who is serious enough to earn it."
Huyen, Independent Cultural Expert & Liaison
