The Heritage Union Journey

Ten stages. One considered path.

Nothing here is automated and nothing is rushed. Every stage is guided by James and Huyen, together.

  1. 1

    A private conversation

    A free, discreet consultation with James to understand your hopes, your circumstances and what a meaningful future looks like for you. No obligation, no pressure.

  2. 2

    Client acceptance

    Not every enquiry becomes a client. We accept those who are serious about companionship, marriage and family — which protects the integrity of the process for everyone involved.

  3. 3

    Your relationship profile

    Together, we build a considered picture of your values, your circumstances and the qualities that matter most to you in a future partner — the foundation for everything that follows.

  4. 4

    Candidate search

    With Huyen's local expertise on the ground in Vietnam, we search for candidates whose values, intentions and circumstances genuinely align with your profile.

  5. 5

    Candidate screening

    Every candidate is met and assessed in person by Huyen — for intention, character and readiness for a serious, long-term relationship. Nothing is taken on trust alone.

  6. 6

    A considered shortlist

    From that screening process, a small, carefully considered shortlist is prepared — never a catalogue of profiles to browse.

  7. 7

    Five curated introductions

    We introduce you to a small number of screened candidates chosen specifically for you — considered, unhurried, and grounded in genuine long-term compatibility.

  8. 8

    Relationship development

    As a connection grows, cultural guidance and concierge support continue — helping both of you navigate distance, language and expectation with confidence.

  9. 9

    A Vietnam visit

    When the time is right, we help coordinate a visit to meet in person — practical arrangements handled discreetly, with Huyen's support on the ground.

  10. 10

    A successful relationship

    Companionship. Marriage. Family. A future built with care, guided every step from the first conversation to the life that follows.

The Heritage Union Trust Framework

Why this process works.

Every stage above is held together by the same principles — selective acceptance, local expertise, founder involvement and support that continues. Transparency and confidentiality run through all of it, from the first email to the wedding day.

Selective acceptance

We do not accept every enquiry. Selective client acceptance protects the seriousness of the process for candidates and clients alike.

Local expertise

Huyen's presence on the ground in Vietnam means every candidate is met and understood in context, not assessed from a distance.

Founder involvement

James is personally involved in every consultation. This is a consultancy led by its founders, not a system that runs without them.

Ongoing support

Guidance does not end at the introduction. We remain involved as a relationship develops, through to marriage and beyond.
